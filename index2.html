PixelClock - Pomodoro Timer for Mac

PixelClock is a Pomodoro timer app designed to help you boost productivity. You can customize your Pomodoro timer with work durations, task details, break times, and long break times.

After completing 4 Pomodoros, PixelClock will automatically trigger a long break, allowing you to relax and recharge.

Key Features:

  • Custom Time Settings: Adjust work time, short break time, and long break time according to your needs.
  • Progress Sync Display: The progress bar in the Mac menu bar synchronizes with the timer.
  • Long Break Reminder: After completing 4 Pomodoros, the app will automatically start a long break.

Feature Overview

How PixelClock Can Improve Your Productivity

Using the Pomodoro Technique has been proven to help people stay focused and efficient. By breaking work into manageable intervals with frequent breaks, you avoid burnout and stay productive longer.

With PixelClock, you can easily track your Pomodoros and ensure you take necessary breaks to stay refreshed and maintain focus throughout your workday.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does the Pomodoro Technique work?

The Pomodoro Technique consists of 25-minute work intervals followed by short 5-minute breaks. After 4 Pomodoros, a longer break of 15-30 minutes is taken to recharge.

Can I customize the timer settings?

Yes, PixelClock allows you to adjust both the work time and the break times to suit your productivity needs.
